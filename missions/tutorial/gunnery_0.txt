<b>Naval Gunnery</b>
You have been assigned command of USS Northampton, a Northampton class heavy cruiser.

Open Main Guns panel by either <KEY:LeftMouse> on the Main Guns button in the toolbar or use <KEY:Weap0>.

<i>FIRING ORDERS DO NOT SHOW WHAT GUNS ARE CURRENTLY DOING, but rather what order you wish to give to the guns.
Mouse over individual gun turret icons to see what they are currently doing.</i>

Manual Firing
1) <KEY:LeftMouse> on the MAN setting to switch to manual firing mode.
2) <KEY:LeftMouse> on the FIRE button. Cursor becomes a weapon aim marker.
3) Drag the marker about 3000 yd to the left of Northampton and <KEY:LeftMouse> on the map.
Main guns will turn and fire at the position you designated.

Experiment by <KEY:LeftMouse> on the FIRE button again and <KEY:LeftMouse> marker at the north edge of the map to fire in that direction; then repeat again for the south and east edges of the map.
<KEY:LeftMouse> on CEASE to stop firing.
