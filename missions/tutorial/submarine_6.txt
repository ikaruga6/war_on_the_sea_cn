Manual Depth Charge Attacks
Depth charges may be dropped manually by sailing over an enemy submarine and doing the following:
1) Use <KEY:Weap4> to open the Depth Charges weapon panel.
2) Use the SETTINGS sliders to set the number of repeats and time interval between repeats.
3) <KEY:LeftMouse> depth charge icons to select one or more to use.
4) Use FIRE to begin dropping depth charges.

Depth Charge Icon
Mouse over the depth charge icon to view number of depth charges remaining.
The number of repeats remaining and progress during each repeat of dropping depth charges are shown on the depth charge icon.

Continue to pursue the enemy submarine until it is sunk or end this exercise when ready.
