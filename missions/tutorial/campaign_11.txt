<b>Map Tools</b>

Ruler:
Use <KEY:MapRuler> and drag to measure the distance and bearing between two points on the map.


Markers:
Use <KEY:MapMarker> to place a time stamped map marker on the map.
Use the text box to add a custom name to the marker.
Use the slider to change the icon of the marker from shapes to
ship or aircraft types to represent what has been marked.
<KEY:LeftMouse> REMOVE to delete a marker.
<KEY:LeftMouse> X in upper right of panel to close marker editor.

<KEY:LeftMouse> on an exiting map marker will open it for editing.
Mouse over a map marker to see its details.
