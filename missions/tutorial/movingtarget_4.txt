<b>Unit Viewer Details</b>
Buttons for statistics, weapons, anti-aircraft capabilities as well as sensors and armour are available.

Statistics
Images of the unit are shown along with physical size, weight, speed and crew. For sea units, the number of ships in the class and their names are available.

Weapons
Lists all available weapon systems on a ship along with detailed statistics such as range and reload times. Dual Purpose refers to guns that can shoot at both surface targets as well as aircraft. A non-dual purpose gun can shoot at surface targets only.

Anti-Aircraft
Shows the firing arcs of anti-aircraft guns.
