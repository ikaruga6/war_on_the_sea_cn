<b>Dogfight At Will</b>
Repeat this training exercise, skipping Step 1.

<KEY:LeftMouse> the colored group tab to select both Wildcats.

Open the Orders Panel (<KEY:Orders>) and <KEY:LeftMouse> on the Dogfight At Will button located under COMBAT.
This will toggle Dogfight At Will on/off for all selected aircraft, note the "guns" icon in the roster tabs when it is on.

When on, Dogfight At Will allows your aircraft to attack any enemy aircraft they detect, without being issued an attack order to do so.
It can be applied to any aircraft with forward firing guns, however larger aircraft without forward guns (such as medium and heavy bombers) cannot dogfight.

Dogfight At Will is on by default for regular battles.
