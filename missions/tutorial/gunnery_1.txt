<b>Splitting Fire</b>
Splitting fire allows guns of the main battery to fire at different targets. To demonstrate this;
1) <KEY:LeftMouse> on the icon of the rear gun to deselect it.
2) <KEY:LeftMouse> on the FIRE button again and <KEY:LeftMouse> on the map about 3000 yd to the west to fire forward guns in that direction.
3) <KEY:LeftMouse> on the two forward gun icons to deselect them
4) <KEY:LeftMouse> on the icon of the rear gun to select it.
5) <KEY:LeftMouse> on the FIRE button again and <KEY:LeftMouse> on the map about 3000 yd to the west to fire rear gun in that direction.
<i>Note CEASE, HOLD and FIRE commands are applied ONLY to the selected gun turrets.</i>

Colored icons corresponding to HOLD or FIRE are displayed on each gun turret icon indicate the current action.
Mouse over of a turret icon for additional information about it, including the firing arc, ammunition and current settings.

Experiment with firing the various guns or aiming (HOLD) in different directions. Remember that a gun can only fire within its firing arc (shown by mouse over turret icon) and within its range. A white target position marker on the firing arc is outside of the arc.

When finished CEASE firing all guns by selecting them all and using CEASE.
