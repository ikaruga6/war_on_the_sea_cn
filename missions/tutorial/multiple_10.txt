<b>Smoke Screens</b>
Be sure you are controlling a destroyer, then <KEY:LeftMouse> colored tab in Unit Roster to select all destroyers.
<KEY:LeftMouse> Lay Smoke button in Orders Panel to begin laying smoke with all 3 destroyers.

- A ship laying smoke or looking at a target laying smoke is always considered to be viewing through smoke.
- Viewing through smoke applies a significant penalty to solution calculations againt the target.
- Enemy ships laying smoke is a good indication that they are attempting to withdraw from combat.
- Firing at ships laying smoke or viewed through a smoke screen may not be a good use of ammuntion.


When ready, end the mission;
1) Either <KEY:LeftMouse> on the Menu button in the toolbar or use <KEY:Menu>.
2) <KEY:LeftMouse> on the QUIT button to end the mission.
