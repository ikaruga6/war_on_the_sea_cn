<b>Weather</b>
Weather is dynamically generated based on meteorological conditions of the region, Trade winds and season.

Use the map filter button (<KEY:ToggleWeather>) to turn on (and off) the weather overlay.

Cloud icons are described under MAP KEY of the Campaign Summary and numbers indicate Sea State (or wind speed over land regions).

- Bad weather reduces the detection ships and aircraft.
- Strong winds increase the chance of fires spreading on board a ship.
- High seas increase the chance of flooding spreading within a ship.
