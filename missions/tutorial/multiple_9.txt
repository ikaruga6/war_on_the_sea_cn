<b>Orders Panel</b>
Open the Orders Panel using (<KEY:Orders>) or the icon in the Main Toolbar.

CONTROLS
Manage navigation and submarine specific commands here; only affect Current Unit.

FORMATION
Manage formation type, spacing and whether to leave a formation for ALL SELECTED UNITS.

COMBAT - Orders issued apply to ALL SELECTED UNITS.
ATTACK/CEASE - start firing at current target or stop firing.
Fire At Will - Ships will automatically manage gunnery and engage enemy ships (or dogfight for fighters).
Auto Fire Flak - dual purpose guns automatically switch to aircraft in range.
Evade Hazards - automatically avoid other ships and land.
Smoke - lay or cease laying smoke screens.
RADAR/SONAR - cease radar/active sonar for all selected units.
