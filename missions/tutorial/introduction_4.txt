<b>Basic Torpedo Attack</b>
Pan camera south to look at target ship (180 on the bearing indicator in top centre of screen).

1) Use <KEY:TargetMode> to enter Target Mode (note the mouse pointer change).
2) While in Target Mode <KEY:LeftMouse> on the target ship to set it as your target.
4) Open Torpedo panel by either <KEY:LeftMouse> on the Torpedo button in the toolbar or use <KEY:Weap2>.
5) Press FIRE to launch torpedoes at the target ship.

Each torpedo mount will fire 1x torpedo (each) with a 0° spread at the target as per the sliders under Settings.

When ready, use next arrow to continue.
